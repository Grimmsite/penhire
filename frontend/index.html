Questions

Frequently Asked

Are the jobs real and verified?

Yes. Employers pay to post jobs, which filters out fake listings. Every job goes live only after payment is confirmed. We also manually review suspicious posts.

How do I receive payment for jobs?

Payment is agreed directly between you and the employer. Most employers pay via PayPal, Wise, or direct bank transfer — all of which connect to M-Pesa via Payoneer or Wise.

What if I unlock a job and don't get it?

Unlock fees are non-refundable, just like applying on any professional platform. This is why we show you the job pay, category, and description summary before you unlock.

How do employers post jobs?

Employers fill in the job form, pay a small posting fee via PayPal or card, and the job goes live instantly. Applications arrive directly to their email.

Can I apply to multiple jobs?

Absolutely. You can unlock and apply to as many jobs as you want. Many writers unlock 3–5 jobs per week and consistently land 1–2 of them.

Is PenHire only for Kenyan writers?

Writers from any African country are welcome. Employers are primarily from the US, UK, Canada, and Australia — all paying in USD.
